    Andy Warhol in Stamps

    Though many may argue that Andy Warhol is one of the modern pop icons, I wanted to take a different approach in depicting the artist. After doing some research, I found that he had a certain duality of his public image and his private self. Namely, he was a Roman Catholic, but he was influential in pop culture. He did screen prints and art on political figures, but he did not express partisan views, leaning more towards apolitical views. Warhol also came from a poor family and rose to celebrity status. These dual sides of Warhol are what I wanted to capture in stamps.
