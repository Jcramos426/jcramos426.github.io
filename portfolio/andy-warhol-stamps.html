    Redesigning MICA Graphic Design Blog

    Designing a newer user experience for reading, I created a book for David Sedaris' "The Cow & the Turkey." In short, Sedaris writes about a selfish cow who gives nothing to her Secret Santa recipient the turkey. When the turkey leaves after the cow reveals the turkey's Christmas dinner fate, it is then that she realizes that as the cow's Secret Santa, the turkey likewise has given nothing in return. Cue a different book design/book art.
