    How to Be a Bad Boss

    Inspired by NBC's 'The Office,' I wanted to create a reverse how-to guide for how to be a bad boss. The project involved creating a folder with three small guides on organization, people skills, and safety. Quotes came directly from the show, and the characters featured include Michael Scott and Dwight Schrute. I enjoyed putting together the content and creating a humorous piece. It was my typography class's favorite piece for the book project.
