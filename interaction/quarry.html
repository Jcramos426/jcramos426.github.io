    Quarry

    The goal was to create a news aggregator app for mobile devices that allows its users to filter through stories and news sources that are more accurate and reliable.

    The App for the Skeptics

    Quarry is for the skeptical users who searches for accurate sources and accurate stories. Bias saturates some news stories while other sources can be truthful. News is like a natural resource to be mined or could also be perceived as a cube with many sides. And for the user who prefers the raw source than a manufactured product, Quarry enables the user to evaluate the truth of a story. The user can filter through the more accurate stories and can rate the credibility/accuracy of a news source/story.
