    The Motorhouse

    The Motorhouse is an existing building in north Baltimore that has stood since its beginnings in the early 20th century. Like Baltimore, it has adapted over the decades, starting as a car sales building and transforming into a building full of independent artists.

      •

    Exterior Design

    The goal was to design wayfinding for The Motor House located on 120 West North Avenue in Baltimore, taking into account the visibility from pedestrian and automobile view.

    The Design

    To have a somewhat retro feel, I decided to use Gotham as the typeface. With aluminum-finished signage, I wanted to create a design that doesn't fight with the brick, but exposes the brick. I was inspired by the history of the building. See my mood board on Pinterest.

    Interior Design

    The mission was to create the directory and room number signage for the Motorhouse interior. Following through with the branding, I created the following signage.

    Signage

    Much like the exterior signage, the interior signage will have an aluminum finish, bolted into the walls for durability. The color palette is also bright since the tenants of the building are part of the art industry.
