    Branding JetBlue

    I did a brand refresher on the airline JetBlue. The refresher would have elements of having a fresher and sleeker perspective. I continue to expand the brand to make traveling easier for the entire family.

    Current Brand

    The current brand of JetBlue seems to lack complexity and thoughtfulness. Below are the logo and the tail design.

    Concept 1

    The first approach involved creating a system for packaging and plane. Deliverables included plane, shower amenities, boarding pass, and luggage tag. The logo is based on the streamline design; patterns of streams allude back to the velocity and stability.

    Concept 2

    As a traveler who's flown countless times, I've wished I could change the flying experience. Having traveled with my nieces, it can be nerve-wracking when one has to also help children check in, get through security, and fly. Subsequently, I wanted to make flying a better family experience.

    Simpli-fly

    Parents and busy travelers can "simpli-fly" their experience with the boarding bracelet. Whether you're 8, 80, or somewhere in between, be a traveler that flies easily. Rip the ticket and wear on your wrist. Children's tickets also indicate guardian information.
