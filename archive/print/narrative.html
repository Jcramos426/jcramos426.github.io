    The Cow & the Turkey

    Print Book Design

    Designing a book for David Sedaris' "The Cow & the Turkey," I took an experimental approach. The short story revolves around a selfish cow and a vindictive turkey. The cow, knowing the turkey is Christmas dinner, goes against the Secret Santa rules and becomes the turkey's Santa. She later finds out that the turkey was, in turn, her Secret Santa, and she's left with nothing.
