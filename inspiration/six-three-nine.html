    Inspiration

    Six 3 Nine

    Six 3 Nine has such a unique layout, which I've tried in the past. The content of Six 3 Nine is probably the only content appropriate for such a layout. The photography and layout work well together in illustrating movement and adding to the high-end workout feel. I also love how when idle the homepage lights the panels one-by-one. Even the second level of pages has a unique layout that scrolls diagonally across the page.
