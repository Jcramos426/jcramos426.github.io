    Inspiration: Bento Box Entertainment

    I enjoyed a previous site from the site of Bento Box Entertainment, and then the designers changed the site. Above is what the site used to look like. It had two branches: Bento Box Entertainment and Bento Box Interactive. I liked how the triangles would move off the pages and move back with the selected content. The site had such a unique character to it and was consistent with their branding.
