    Lighthouse Brewing

    Lighthouse Brewing also uses parallax, navigation, and animations to produce a great user experience. The color palette of the site also adds to the site in a great way. I may or may not like the kraken... I don't know why I like nautical landscapes, but I do. The site is one of my all-time favorite inspiration sites because it truly balances the excitement with the content. The animations and loading effects provide a good sense of rhythm across the site that is consistent with the site's tone and concept.
