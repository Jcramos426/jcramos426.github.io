    Inspiration: Massis Tea

    Massis Tea offers a vibrant website with visuals and a taste of what the organization produces. I enjoy seeing the vividness and content in a creative way. The site reminds me of Honest Tea, which is good because I like Honest Tea. The site's navigation bar scrolls down to the specific section detailing the service and products. Overall, its use of typography and imagery create a good user experience.
