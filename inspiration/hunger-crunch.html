    Hunger Crunch

    Hunger Crunch features great illustrations, adding to its game theme and mission. The site inspires its users to play the game and help Rice Bowls feed orphaned children. This is my favorite of my favorite internet things. The parallax illustration and title are so playful and tempts user to scroll further. The site also has fun menu buttons like a computer key or game controller.
