    Inspiration

    Liquid Courage

    The first time I came across the site, I did not read the entire thing. The visuals of the infographic was way too exciting for me to read the text. The Nature Conservancy's Liquid Courage site utilizes parallax navigation for this great infographic website. Sure, I don't have the attention for parallax navigation, but I enjoy seeing social design sites like this. Ideally, I hope to create a site that is similar, but it balances content with the excitement well.
