    Lobagola

    Lobagola features rotating navigation. With the warm tones of the website, the site is very inviting and achieves its mission of warming up to its users and possible customers. Its friendliness is also found in the tone of photography and typography. At the same time, the site's homepage should probably be more representative about what Lobagola is. Overall, however, the site has a fresh take on navigation.
