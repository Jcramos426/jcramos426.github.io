    Inspiration

    The Webby Awards

    The Webby Awards is a great example of complicated navigation, showcasing the best of the Internet by the year. Scrolling by year and then section enables smoother navigation. Although I probably won't ever use such complicated navigation, it's a great site for web design inspiration. From mobile sites to websites and film to media, the Webby Awards enables web designers and entrepreneurs to showcase their talent and services. Here, I can find sites regarding great navigation, design, activism, and entrepreneurship.
