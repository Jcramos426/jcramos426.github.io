About

Jenae Ramos is a graphic designer at the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). She is the career development graduate assistant at The Joseph Meyerhoff Center for Career Development and is also a Post-Baccalaureate Graphic Design student. On a side note, she is a foodie, traveler, and a proud member of Save the Manatee Club. She even has a manatee named Chessie, who was found in the Chesapeake Bay in July 1994. If you happen to have any updates on her long-lost manatee, please feel to reach out.
