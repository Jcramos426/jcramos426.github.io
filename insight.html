    Insight

    Challenge

    Designers strive to share not only their final designs, but also their creative process. Unfortunately, many portfolio websites focus on the final designs rather than the process.

    Solution

    Collaborating with other designers from the Design Orlando Meetup, we designed a process portfolio website in which designers can share their process with fellow designers and employers and critique their fellow designers' work.
